The Bored Genius
Light Yagami is an exceptionally intelligent high school student living in Japan. Despite his academic success and a respected family background—his father being a police officer—Light feels deeply dissatisfied with the world. Crime, corruption, and injustice dominate the news every day. To him, society feels rotten and beyond saving. Though admired by everyone around him, Light secretly believes the world needs someone powerful enough to cleanse it and create a new era of justice.
The Death Note
During a normal school day, Light notices something strange falling from the sky into the courtyard outside his school. After classes, curiosity drives him to investigate. Lying on the ground is a mysterious black notebook with the words “Death Note” written on its cover. Initially assuming it’s some kind of prank or abandoned prop, Light picks it up and begins reading the instructions written inside.
The First Rule
Inside the notebook, Light finds a set of chilling rules. The most important one states: “The human whose name is written in this note shall die.” The notebook also explains conditions for how deaths can occur and that the writer must know the person’s face. Though the rules are extremely detailed, Light dismisses them as a strange joke. Still, curiosity pushes him to test whether the notebook truly possesses the deadly power it claims.
Justice Begins
To test the notebook, Light writes the name of a criminal currently being broadcast on the news. According to the rules, the person should die of a heart attack within forty seconds. Light waits, convinced nothing will happen. But moments later, the news reports that the criminal has suddenly collapsed and died. Shocked and terrified, Light realizes that the Death Note’s power is real.
The Shinigami Ryuk
Shortly after using the Death Note several times, Light encounters the true owner of the notebook: Ryuk, a Shinigami—or death god—from another world. Ryuk explains that he dropped the Death Note into the human world simply because he was bored. Now he plans to stay and watch how Light uses the notebook’s power. While Ryuk has no intention of helping or stopping him, he is clearly amused by the chaos Light’s actions may cause.
The Birth of Kira
After confirming the notebook’s power, Light makes a radical decision. He will use the Death Note to eliminate criminals and reshape the world into a place free of evil. As criminals begin dying mysteriously across the globe, people start noticing the pattern. Soon the media and the public give the unseen judge a name: Kira, derived from the word “killer.”
A World Divided
Kira’s actions quickly spark worldwide debate. Some people celebrate him as a savior who is finally delivering justice where the legal system failed. Others fear that Kira is nothing more than a mass murderer abusing supernatural power. Governments and law enforcement agencies around the world begin searching desperately for the identity of the mysterious killer.
The Detective L
To capture Kira, the world’s greatest detective enters the investigation. Known only by the alias L, he is a mysterious figure whose true identity and appearance remain secret. Despite the limited information available, L quickly begins analyzing the pattern of Kira’s killings and devising strategies to expose him.
L's Clever Trap
L sets a brilliant trap to test Kira’s abilities. A man claiming to be L appears on television and publicly challenges Kira. Light immediately kills him using the Death Note. Moments later, the real L reveals that the broadcast only aired in one region of Japan. By reacting so quickly, Kira unknowingly revealed his location.
Internal Investigation
As the investigation intensifies, suspicion begins narrowing toward certain individuals connected to law enforcement families. Light’s father is part of the task force searching for Kira, which places Light dangerously close to the center of the investigation. To avoid suspicion and control the investigation from within, Light joins the task force himself.
Battle of Geniuses
Now working together on the same investigation, Light and L constantly observe and test one another. Both are aware the other could be Kira, yet neither has the evidence needed to prove it. Their rivalry becomes a psychological war of strategy, manipulation, and deduction as they attempt to outsmart each other.
The Second Kira
The investigation takes a surprising turn when another person claiming to be Kira begins killing criminals. This second Kira uses a different approach and appears to possess knowledge the original Kira does not. The existence of a second Death Note complicates the investigation and creates new opportunities for Light.
Misa Amane
The second Kira is revealed to be Misa Amane, a famous model and idol. After making a deal with a Shinigami, she possesses the Shinigami Eyes, allowing her to see anyone’s real name and lifespan. Misa deeply admires Kira and becomes completely devoted to Light, offering to help him achieve his vision of a new world.
Forgotten Power
When suspicion against him becomes overwhelming, Light takes a drastic step. He gives up ownership of the Death Note, causing him to lose all memories related to it. Without those memories, Light behaves like a completely different person and genuinely works to capture Kira alongside L.
Memories Regained
During a carefully orchestrated operation involving another Death Note user, Light finally touches the notebook again while riding in a helicopter. The moment his hand makes contact with it, all his memories instantly return. With perfect timing, Light continues the plan he secretly prepared before giving up the notebook.
The Silence of the Bell
Through complex manipulation involving another Death Note user and a Shinigami, Light finally manages to eliminate his greatest rival. L dies before proving Light’s guilt. After years of intense rivalry, the greatest detective in the world falls—leaving Light victorious.
The Perfect Victory
After L’s death, Light privately celebrates his success. Standing at his rival’s grave, he reflects on how he outmaneuvered the one person capable of defeating him. Declaring it his “perfect victory,” Light laughs triumphantly while Ryuk silently watches. Even the Shinigami seems surprised by how far Light’s ambition and ruthlessness have evolved.
Shadows of the Past
Several years pass, and Kira’s influence grows stronger than ever. Crime rates fall dramatically as criminals fear the mysterious judge who can kill from anywhere in the world. Many people now worship Kira as the god of a new world.
The Successors
Despite Light’s apparent victory, the legacy of L continues. Two young prodigies raised to succeed him—Near and Mello—take up the investigation. Each uses different methods to pursue the truth behind Kira.
The Goddess's Voice
Light secretly recruits Kiyomi Takada, a respected news anchor and his former university acquaintance. Acting as Kira’s spokesperson to the public, she spreads Kira’s ideology and strengthens his influence across the world.
Sacrifice for the Cause
When Takada risks exposing critical information about Kira, Light takes drastic action. Using a hidden piece of the Death Note, he forces her into a situation where she ultimately dies in a burning building. The incident demonstrates just how ruthless Light has become in protecting his identity.
The Hand of God
Teru Mikami emerges as one of Kira’s most loyal followers. Completely devoted to Kira’s ideology, he acts as Kira’s executioner by carrying out killings using the Death Note. To him, Kira is not just a leader but a divine figure bringing justice to the world.
The Final Confrontation
Near patiently studies Mikami’s behavior and realizes he may be the key to exposing Kira. By secretly replacing the real Death Note with a fake one, Near prepares a final trap designed to reveal Kira’s identity in front of witnesses.
The Final Page
In the final confrontation at an abandoned warehouse, Near reveals the truth: Light Yagami is Kira. With the fake Death Note exposed, Light’s elaborate plans collapse. Cornered and desperate, he attempts to escape his fate, marking the dramatic end of Kira’s reign.
THE END
